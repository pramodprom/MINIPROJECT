About Us

Zomato, founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, is a leading global restaurant discovery, food delivery, and dining out platform. Headquartered in Gurugram, India, Zomato connects millions of food enthusiasts with their favorite cuisines, offering a seamless experience for discovering and enjoying food.

With a presence in over 25 countries, Zomato bridges the gap between customers and restaurants, providing services like online ordering, restaurant reviews, table reservations, and Zomato Pro, a premium membership offering exclusive dining benefits. Their technology-driven approach ensures convenience and efficiency for both diners and restaurant partners.
